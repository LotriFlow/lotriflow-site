Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy matters. LotriFlow Quit is designed to keep all your personal data on your device. We don't collect, store, or share your health information.

Our Privacy Commitment

LotriFlow ("we", "our", or "us") operates the LotriFlow Quit mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Don't Collect

LotriFlow Quit is built with privacy at its core. We do not collect:

  • Your quit date or smoking history
  • Health milestone progress
  • Craving logs or breathing exercise usage
  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes

Local Data Storage

All your quit smoking data—including your quit date, cigarettes per day, cost per pack, and progress—is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Apple Watch Sync

If you use the LotriFlow Quit Apple Watch companion app, your data is synchronized between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's secure WatchConnectivity framework. This synchronization happens directly between your devices and does not pass through our servers.

In-App Purchases

LotriFlow Quit offers optional Pro features through Apple's In-App Purchase system. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We receive only anonymized purchase confirmations necessary to unlock Pro features—we never see your payment details, Apple ID, or billing information.

Intelligent Coach Feature

The Intelligent Coach feature (available to Pro users) may process your questions to provide personalized support. Any conversation data used for this feature is processed in accordance with our AI provider's privacy practices and is not stored or used for any other purpose.

Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated analytics to understand app usage patterns and improve our service. This data cannot be used to identify individual users and includes only general metrics like feature usage counts and crash reports.

Third-Party Services

LotriFlow Quit may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases
  • Apple HealthKit: Optional integration if you choose to share data with Apple Health (your choice, not automatic)

Data Deletion

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. To delete your LotriFlow Quit data:

  • Use the "Reset Progress" option in the app settings, or
  • Delete the LotriFlow Quit app from your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
